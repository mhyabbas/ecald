Courses

The development of CALD cultural competencies in the health sector include the recognition of culture as a determinant of health status; and the recognition of the need for a culturally competent workforce to address issues of equity and health disparities of CALD groups. CALD cultural competence focuses on the skills, behaviours and attitudes required for working with culturally, linguistically and religiously diverse groups.

eCALD offers a range of face-to-face and online courses for both caring for culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) patients as well as working in a CALD workforce. With all course bases on cultural awareness, sensitivity, knowledge and practical skills.

In this section you can access information about the variety of courses offered by eCALD® in both online and face-to-face

Find out more about each of our courses in the sections below

Working with CALD patients

Provide learners with insight and understanding on cultural dimensions, how different cultures respond to health related situations, different health concepts, faith-based practices, and differences in expectations from health systems.

Culturally diverse workplaces
