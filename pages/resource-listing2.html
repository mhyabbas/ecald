About us

eCALD® services is a division of the Institute for Innovation and Improvement (i3) of Waitemata DHB. eCALD® services have developed e-learning and face-to-face courses as well as online, print and HTML cross-cultural resources to address the lack of CALD cultural competence training and information for the New Zealand health workforce.

The provision of eCALD® courses and resources is funded by the Ministry of Health as part of the Auckland Migrant Settlement Strategy Programme. .
